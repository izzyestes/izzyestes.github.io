Isabel Estes

About

Isabel Estes is a vocalist from Phoenix, Arizona, currently residing in Tempe, Arizona. She is an undergraduate student at Barrett, the Honors College at Arizona State University working towards a Bachelor's in Digital Culture. Isabel is passionate about music and technology, where they can lead us in the future. Please contact if you'd like to collaborate on a project!




Education

Isabel attended Xavier College Preparatory from 2014 until 2018 when she graduated with honors and with an Associate's Degree from Rio Salado College, obtained through dual enrollment. She has been attending Barrett, the Honors College at Arizona State University since Fall 2018 and is on track to graduate by Spring 2022.

Experience

Isabel worked as a Hostess and as a Server at Sushi Brokers, LLC for a year until the current pandemic. She now has a job at Duck Á Decanter. Being in customer service for so long has taught her valuable lessons about teamwork, organization, thinking quickly on your feet, leadership, and creating a positive work environment.

Skills

Isabel is a very organized, efficient, positive person with a good work ethic. Her skills include but are not limited to: basic knowledge of Spanish and French, Microsoft Office, Swift, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and experience with several music, video, and photo editing softwares (GarageBand, Audacity, Adobe Audition, Adobe Premier Pro, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, etc.).
